Translation

Searches can use letters, commas, and spaces to translate one or many words. Search request with numbers or any other character will not be accepted.
Here are some examples of what works:

  • translate these words
  • translate,these,words
  • translate, these, words
  • translate , these , words
  • translate

You can really just enter the words however you want and it (hopefully) shouldn't break.


Other Stuff

The translation results show up in cards that can be dragged and dropped. The minus button on the top right of a card minimizes its translation. The x button removes the translation.
